Shivaji Maharaj

In 1674, Shivaji was crowned as the king despite opposition from local Brahmins. Shivaji employed people of all castes and religions, including Muslims[13]Europeans, in his administration and armed forces.[14] Over the course of his life, Shivaji engaged in both alliances and hostilities with the Mughal Empire the Sultanate of Golconda Nagar, the Sultanate of Bijapur and the European colonial powers. Shivaji's military forces expanded the Maratha sphere of influence, capturing and building forts, and forming a Maratha navy.
