Heloo..Welcome To Pune

The old name for Pune was Punya-vishaya or Punaka, according to a Rashtrakuta Dynasty inscription from 937 CE. Other old names include Punyapur, Punakavishaya, Poona, and Kasba Pune. The name was officially changed from Poona to Pune in 1978.

Pune is particularly famous for its Paithani sarees, Kolhapuri chappals, and delicious snacks like Laxmi Narayan Chiwda and Shrewsbury biscuits.
